About Us

At Rapid M3, we try to comprehend clients' emerging needs and actively partner with the clients to make their businesses stronger, more productive and more profitable. Working closely with stakeholders gives us better insights into their business, allowing us to translate that into providing the best possible IT services and solutions to their business challenges.

Rapid M3 operates its development facility mainly in Sri Lanka. The facility allows Rapid M3 to leverage the highly literate talent pool available in the country, while in turn helping clients significantly improve their competitiveness in the marketplace. In addition to our co-founders, our engineering team consists of highly experienced and specialized industry experts driven by a passion for delivering the best possible outcome that meets and quite often exceeds customer business needs.
